Bramley apple pie, salted caramel, walnuts, vanilla ice cream

Preparation method:

Method: the pie comes in a dish, the apple compote is covered with a puff pastry top, and finished with a caramel sauce and chopped walnuts
Apple Compote: the bramley apples are slow cooked with caster sugar, ground cinnamon, brandy and a bit of flour.
Bramley apple: A variety of apple, which usually eaten cooked due to its sourness, some people may have it raw as a palate cleanser, when it`s cooked it has lighter flavour, becomes golden and fluffy
Sea Salt Caramel Sauce: sugar, cream vanilla, sea salt & butter
Allergens:
Bramley apple pie: gluten
Caramel: Milk
Vanilla ice cream: eggs, milk
Walnuts: Nuts (CAN BE DONE WITHOUT)
THE DISH CAN BE DONE VEGAN WITH NO ICE CREAM (there is no diary in used in the pastry)
